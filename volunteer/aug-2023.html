MISSION SNAPSHOT
A YEAR OF FAITHFUL SERVICE
Trinity started volunteering with her church preparing meals for men in our recovery program at Riverton Place. Later, Trinity began serving women in our recovery program at Hope Place, and joining her family on Search + Rescue. Trinity’s parents demonstrated that serving people in need is a core value of their family. They especially enjoy connecting with guests and staff through meaningful conversations. Through their service, they’ve developed a greater perspective and learned to see people as God sees them, beloved and each one with a story.
Not only does Trinity serve at Hope Place and go out with Search + Rescue, but she’s also sorted donations at the Distribution Center, earning her the exclusive “Trifecta” volunteer pin, which recognizes people who have volunteered in those three areas. When volunteers serve in different areas, they gain a greater understanding of the Mission as a whole, including its impact, its challenges, and culture.
If you are inspired to experience volunteering in different areas of the Mission as Trinity has, visit ugm.org/volunteer to sign up for your next opportunity.
NOURISH LIVES AND BUILD RELATIONSHIPS!
Vested Volunteers have filled some very specific needs that have arisen over the past couple of months. God’s timing and provision (through you!) has been such a gift to the Mission!
In our Learning & Education Center, our guests needed help with both basic computer skills and GED preparation. There was also a need for volunteers to work in the computer labs on weekends.
Our Children’s Ministry (FORT) needed more volunteers to serve, teach, and celebrate our growing population of children.
We needed new teachers to lead GriefShare, A Parenting Class, and Experiencing God.
We had (and still have!) a need for Christian mentors to serve our guests in discipleship relationships.
In each case, Vested Volunteers stepped up and answered God’s call to meet the need. We have more opportunities to share with you! If God gives you a desire to use your gifts to serve in a deeper way, get in touch with us at volunteer@ugm.org or (206) 723-0767. We may very well have just the opportunity you’re looking for (or close to it!), and we’d love to get you plugged in to ongoing service opportunities which meet the needs of our guests, staff, and homeless neighbors. As a Vested Volunteer, you can be a big part of what God is doing through the Mission to bring the love of Jesus and hope for a new life to our neighbors.
